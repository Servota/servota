# Servota
Simple rostering and painless replacements.

## Audience & Promise
For volunteer groups and shift-based teams—churches, clubs, workplaces, and families—**Servota** gives you a clear roster in minutes and makes swaps a one-tap action. Create roles and weekly/monthly templates, auto-generate shifts, assign only eligible, available people, and let the app handle replacements and reminders. Members see a clean “My Roster” and set unavailability; schedulers get a fast grid and zero double-bookings.

- **Fast setup, zero fuss:** Roles → templates → shifts, live in minutes.  
- **Painless replacements:** Tap “Can’t make it”; eligible people are notified and the first to accept is auto-assigned.  
- **Fewer no-shows:** Conflict checks, unavailability, and timely reminders keep everyone in sync.
